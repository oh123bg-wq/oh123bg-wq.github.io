About The World of Pokemon

Pokémon is a world-renowned Japanese media franchise managed by The Pokémon Company, a consortium between Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures. It was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1996, originally appearing as a pair of video games for the Game Boy. Since then, it has expanded into a massive ecosystem of movies, merchandise, and a highly competitive Trading Card Game (TCG).

The story typically follows a Pokémon Trainer who travels across various regions to capture and train diverse creatures known as Pokémon. These trainers participate in battles to earn Gym Badges and eventually challenge the Pokémon League. Along the way, they often encounter legendary creatures and thwart the plans of criminal organizations like Team Rocket.

The anime adaptation, which debuted in 1997, famously followed the adventures of Ash Ketchum (Satoshi) and his partner Pikachu for over 25 years. The series is celebrated globally for its themes of friendship, its iconic creature designs, and its deep strategic gameplay that continues to captivate millions of fans and collectors worldwide.
